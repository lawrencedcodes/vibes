## Introduction

Welcome to the Tech Career Guidance Web Application! This platform is designed to provide clear, actionable pathways into technology careers for individuals with diverse backgrounds. Whether you're considering a career change, looking to upskill, or just exploring your options in the tech industry, this application will help you navigate your journey.

## Getting Started

### Registration and Login

1. Visit the application homepage
2. Click on "Register" to create a new account
3. Fill in your details (email, username, password)
4. Verify your email address through the link sent to your inbox
5. Log in with your credentials

### Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you'll be directed to your dashboard, which provides:
- A summary of your assessment progress
- Your recommended career paths
- Your current learning plan progress
- Recent community activity
- Upcoming expert Q&A sessions

## User Assessment

The assessment process is divided into several sections to gather comprehensive information about your interests, skills, and preferences.

### Interest & Passion Exploration

- Answer questions about your interests in different technology domains
- Rate your enthusiasm for various tech-related activities
- Identify problem-solving approaches that resonate with you

### Skill & Strength Assessment

- Evaluate your existing technical skills
- Assess your soft skills and transferable abilities
- Identify your learning style and preferences

### Work Style & Preferences

- Determine your preferred work environment (remote, hybrid, office)
- Identify your ideal team dynamics
- Assess your work-life balance priorities

### Job Role Interest

- Explore different tech job roles
- Rate your interest in specific positions
- Identify industry preferences

### Technological Access

- Specify your computer and internet access
- Indicate your available time for learning
- Note any accessibility requirements

## Career Recommendations

Based on your assessment, the application will generate personalized career recommendations.

### Recommendation Details

Each recommendation includes:
- Job role title and description
- Match score showing compatibility with your profile
- Detailed reasons for the recommendation
- Salary range and market demand information
- Required skills and growth opportunities
- Day-to-day responsibilities

### Exploring Career Paths

- Click on any recommended career to view detailed information
- Use the "Compare" feature to evaluate different career options
- Save careers to your favorites for future reference
- Generate a 1-year learning plan for any selected career

## Learning Plans

The application creates personalized 1-year learning plans for your chosen career path.

### Plan Structure

Each learning plan is divided into four phases:
1. **Foundation Phase (Months 1-3)**: Building fundamental skills and knowledge
2. **Building Phase (Months 4-6)**: Advancing technical skills and collaborative projects
3. **Specialization Phase (Months 7-9)**: Developing specialized expertise and industry-relevant projects
4. **Career Preparation Phase (Months 10-12)**: Networking, resume building, and job search preparation

### Milestones and Progress Tracking

- Weekly and monthly milestones with specific learning objectives
- Progress tracking for each milestone (Not Started, In Progress, Completed)
- Resource recommendations for each milestone
- Adjustable timeline based on your available learning hours
